TBD 15625 Ways – Hold & Spin Slot Game
=======================================

Grid:         6 Reels × 5 Rows
Ways:         15,625 (5^6)
RTP Versions: 92% / 94% / 96%
Volatility:   Medium-High
Max Win:      5,000x Bet

SYMBOL SET
----------
Low:    10, J, Q, K, A (metal carved)
Mid:    LAN (Lantern), HEL (Helmet), PIC (Pickaxe), TNT (TNT Barrel)
High:   GCA (Gold Cart), DIA (Diamond Cluster), DRI (Drill Machine)

Special:
  W  - Wild (Explosive Charge) - reels 2, 3, 4 only. Substitutes all except S and M.
  S  - Scatter (Mine Entrance Gate) - triggers Free Spins (3=10, 4=15, 5=20 FS)
  M  - Money Symbol (Gold Nugget) - triggers Hold & Spin with 6+ on screen.

HOLD & SPIN
-----------
Trigger:  6+ Money symbols anywhere
Respins:  3 (reset to 3 on new money symbol)
Full Grid (30 symbols): GRAND jackpot (1000x)

Money Symbol Values (base):
  1x (40%), 2x (25%), 3x (15%), 5x (10%), 10x (6%), 20x (2%), 50x (0.5%)
  Jackpot chance: 0.3% per Money symbol

Jackpots (fixed):
  MINI: 20x, MINOR: 50x, MAJOR: 200x, GRAND: 1000x (full grid only)

FREE SPINS
----------
Trigger:  3 Scatters = 10 FS, 4 = 15 FS, 5 = 20 FS
Retrigger: 3 = +5, 4 = +10, 5 = +15

Enhancements:
  - Money symbols minimum 2x value
  - Hold & Spin can trigger inside Free Spins
  - Multiplier grows +1 per respin round during FS Hold & Spin (capped at 10x)
  - 5% chance of random fully wild reel per spin
